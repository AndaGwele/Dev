Our Process

From Idea to Launch, Step by Step

We follow a proven, structured process that keeps you informed and involved at every stage. No guesswork, no surprises.

Step 01

Discovery

We start by understanding your business, goals, audience, and requirements. This is where we learn what makes your organisation unique and what you need from your digital solution.

  • Initial consultation call
  • Requirements gathering
  • Competitor and market research
  • Technical feasibility assessment

Step 02

Proposal

Based on what we learn, we put together a clear proposal outlining the scope, timeline, features, and investment. No surprises, no hidden costs.

  • Detailed project scope
  • Feature breakdown
  • Timeline and milestones
  • Transparent pricing

Step 03

Design

We create wireframes and visual designs for your project. You will see exactly how your website or system will look and feel before any code is written.

  • Wireframes and layouts
  • Visual design mockups
  • Branding alignment
  • Your feedback and revisions

Step 04

Development

Our developers bring the approved designs to life using clean, well-structured code. We build with reliability, performance, and future scalability in mind.

  • Clean, semantic code
  • Responsive implementation
  • Database and backend setup
  • Regular progress updates

Step 05

Testing

Every feature is thoroughly tested across devices and browsers to ensure everything works perfectly. We fix any issues before launch.

  • Cross-browser testing
  • Mobile responsiveness checks
  • Performance optimisation
  • User acceptance testing

Step 06

Delivery

We deploy your project to your live environment, set up hosting and domains, and make sure everything is running smoothly for your launch.

  • Deployment to live server
  • Domain and hosting setup
  • SSL certificate configuration
  • Launch checklist completion

Step 07

Maintenance

After launch, we continue to support you with updates, fixes, and improvements. Your website stays secure, fast, and up to date.

  • Ongoing technical support
  • Regular updates and patches
  • Performance monitoring
  • Feature enhancements
