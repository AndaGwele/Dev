About Us

Building Digital Solutions for South African Businesses

We are a passionate team of developers and designers dedicated to helping small businesses, startups, and organisations succeed in the digital world.

Our Story

From Small Beginnings to Big Impact

Anda Gwele Development was founded with a simple mission: to make high-quality web development accessible to small businesses and organisations across South Africa. We noticed that many local businesses were being left behind in the digital revolution, either because they couldn't afford expensive agency fees or because they didn't know where to start.

That's why we built our company around three core principles: affordability, quality, and partnership. We believe every business deserves a professional online presence, regardless of size or budget. Our team works closely with each client to understand their unique needs and deliver solutions that truly make a difference.

Today, we've helped over 30 businesses and organisations establish themselves online, from local shops and restaurants to student organisations and NGOs. Each project is a partnership, and your success is our success.
